What is fringe scenario?

Fringe: identify possible scenarios of use or misuse that push the limits of your system. In general these should not be about technical issues (e.g. network failure) but about use cases that arise in ordinary (but atypical) circumstances that pose issues for the idealized use/functionality you have implemented. For all scenarios, describe what context your users are in (at home, at work, in transit), who they are with (or not with), what help/instructions or other resources they need to find or have at hand. Scenarios should always have a problematic aspect.

Video Production Tips

Plan Your productionInfo

No matter what event you videotape, have a plan. Write down or make a mental list of shorts you will want to include.

Shoot CloseInfo

Normally, you should typically shoot close on your subjects. The more back distractions you have in your shots, the less appealing it will be.

Change angles and positionsInfo

Get in the habit of shooting from different angles and positions. Try to not to stuck in the same place for the entire recording.

Check Sound and RecordingInfo

Be sure to constantly check that audio level to make sure it is not too high or low. Other than that, also learn to recognize when your camcorder is actually recording and when it's not.

Edit Your ProductionInfo

Editing your video shots using what is called a nonlinear editor, such as iMovie, Windows Movie Maker, or Adobe Premiere Elements, is simply the best way to make better video productions.
